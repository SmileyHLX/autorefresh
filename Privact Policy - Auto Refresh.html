Privacy Policy for Auto Refresh Extension

Last updated: June 11, 2025

Data We Collect

The extension accesses the URLs and titles of your active browser tabs to manage auto-refresh timers.
It also stores your preferred refresh intervals locally for each tab.

Why We Collect Data

This data is necessary for the extension to function properly — to refresh tabs automatically based on your settings and to keep track of active auto-refresh sessions.

How We Use Your Data

Data is stored locally in your browser’s storage.
It is never shared, sold, or transferred to any third parties.
Data is used solely to provide the extension’s auto-refresh features.

What We Don’t Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personally identifiable information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Financial, health, or authentication data
  • Personal communications (emails, texts, chats)
  • Location information
  • Browsing history beyond your open tabs
  • Keystrokes, clicks, or other user activity tracking

Your Rights

You can disable or remove the extension at any time. All data related to the extension is removed when the extension is uninstalled.
